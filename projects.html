Motorola Solutions High School Mentorship Program

This application helped automate many administrative tasks Motorola staff previously took countless hours during their high school mentorship program. The app also acts a way of allowing students to upload all of their information in a user friendly format. We also implemented an automatic email system that would give students emails at certain points in the intake process. The web app also allowed volunteers to register as well, giving all of the information in 1 place for the Motorola admins.

P4H Global Mobile App

P4H Global is a non profit that works with teachers in Haiti to help support the education of youth. They were using WhatsApp as their method of communication, but required a more in depth application to handle exchanging info, videos, lesson plans and general messaging. This video is the design demo we presented to them, but the final draft of the application is currently being worked on.
